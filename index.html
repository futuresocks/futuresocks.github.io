I'm a makeup artist based in Glasgow and would love to help you create a look that gives you confidence, and gets you ready for any wedding, special occasion or ‘just because’ day. I'm passionate about using makeup to enhance natural beauty and encouraging skin to really glow - its about changing confidence, not you. I want to help women feel incredible, but also, ultimately, like themselves.

I trained at Sara Hill's prestigious Academy of Makeup in Glasgow on their Pro course and was tutored, and inspired, by some of the best working make up artists in the country.

I am registered with BABTAC (British Association of Beauty Therapy and Cosmetology) which ensures I'm qualified, verified and insured.
